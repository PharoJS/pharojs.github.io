Success Stories

PharoJS is intended for real world applications. We provide here a list of projects that we are aware of. Please let us know (Twitter @pharojs) if your app is not on the list.

  • Farmers Market: Mobile app for both iOS and Android, based on Apache Cordova. Provides Geo-coding and GPS navigation based on Google Maps.
  • MatterJsDemo: This demo shows that PharoJS can integrate JS code from a third-party library. In this case, the library is the MatterJS physics engine
  • Other apps are under development and will be listed here soon.
